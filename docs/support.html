Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first estimate?

Tap the "+" button on the Estimates tab, fill in your client details, add items to each tier (Good, Better, Best), and tap Preview to see your estimate. You can then share it as a PDF.

What's included in the free version?

The free version lets you create estimates with up to 3 items per tier, generate PDF estimates, and share them with clients. It's perfect for getting started.

What does Pro unlock?

Pro unlocks unlimited items per tier, custom business branding on estimates, iCloud sync across devices, digital signature capture, and advanced PDF templates. It's a one-time purchase, not a subscription.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." Your Pro status will be restored on any device using the same Apple ID.

How does iCloud sync work?

When you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your estimates, clients, and product library automatically sync across all your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account.

How do I add my signature to estimates?

In the Pro version, you can sign estimates directly on your device using your finger or Apple Pencil. Tap the signature area in the estimate editor to add your signature.
